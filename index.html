Walkthrough

Players will enter a VR garage from the real world after rolling up the door. They can then walk, turn, and teleport freely and smoothly in the garage. Players will lift the car up and down using a machine and:

  • Open the door and place the driver's seat
  • Grab and install the front/rear bumpers and the exhaust
  • Assemble the engine and fix it in the back trunk
  • Use a professional machine to balance the wheel and install tires
  • Start the engine after all these installations
    •
Players can also toggle a virtual panel to check their progress.

Controls

  • Primary/Secondary Hand Trigger: Interactions - Select, Hold, Grab.
  • Right(Secondary) Thumbstick Left/Right: Rotate View.
  • Right(Secondary) Thumbstick Up: Teleport.
  • Left(Primary) Thumbstick: Walk(Simple Movement).
  • Hand Trigger + B/A Button: Lift the car up/down while on selection.
  • X Button: Display/Undisplay Game UI.
  • X+Y Buttons: Reset Game.
  • A+B Buttons: Cheat Code to complete all tasks.
